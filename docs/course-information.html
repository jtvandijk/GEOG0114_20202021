Useful additional resources

Besides the mandatory and recommended reading for this course, there are some additional resources that are worth checking out:

  1. The command line. The Missing Semester of Your CS Education: MIT’s introduction course on mastering the command line.

  2. Some more command line. explainshell.com; for when you cannot figure it out with MIT’s introduction.

  3. Regular expressions. regexr.com; an online tool to develop and check your regular expressions.

  4. Mapmaking. colorbrewer 2.0; an online tool to select colour palettes for your maps and visualisations.
